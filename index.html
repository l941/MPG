Most Popular Games of 2020

Here is a list of the "Most Popular Games in 2020"

1: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare


The game takes place in a realistic and modern setting. The campaign follows a CIA officer and British SAS forces as they team up with rebels from the fictional country of Urzikstan, combating together against Russian forces who have invaded the country.

2: NBA 2k20


This basketball simulation game, based on the National Basketball Association, is published by 2K Sports.

3: Madden NFL 20


This football simulation game published by Electronic Arts is based on the National Football League.

4: Borderlands 3


Borderlands 3 is a first-person shooter action game; it shipped more than 5 million copies in its first five days.

5: Mortal Kombat 11


This fighting game published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment, is the 11th overall installment in the Mortal Kombat series, following Mortal Kombat X, which came out in 2015.

6: Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order


The game takes place in the universe of Star Wars, following Jedi Padawan as he tries to complete his Jedi training and restore the Jedi Order.

7: Super Smash Bros


Released in 2018, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is the fifth game released in the Super Smash Bros. series.

8: Kingdom Hearts III


Kingdom Hearts III is set against various Disney and Pixar worlds. It’s the 12th installment in the Kingdom Hearts series, meant to wrap up the narrative arc that began with the first game.

9: Tom Clancy's The Division 2


The sequel to 2016’s The Division, this game is set in Washington D.C. as smallpox has decimated the city.

10: Mario Kart 8 Deluxe


As its name suggests, Mario Kart 8 is a kart-racing video game and the eighth installment in the series.

11: Fortnite


It has three different modes: Battle Royale, Save the World, and Creative. Because it’s an online active shooter game, it can be controversial among parents.

12: Minecraft


The sandbox game allows players to move through and build structures in a pixelated, block-like world.

13: Roblox


Roblox is a massively popular multiplayer online game in which players build their own virtual worlds. It was released back in 2006.

14: Super Mario Odyessey


The game follows the characters Mario and his hat spirit, Cappy, on their adventures in attempts to save Princess Peach.

15: Grand Theft Auto V


As its title implies, players in the game’s fictional world of San Andreas commit crimes and try to evade authorities.

16: Marvel's Spider-Man


The game follows the story of both the Peter Parker and Spider-Man sides of the character fighting evil while navigating civilian life.

17: Rocket League


Rocket League is a soccer-like game in which the players are actually cars powered by rockets.

18: League of Legends


The multiplayer online battle game League of Legends came out in 2009 and its popularity has only soared since, gaining viral strength.

19: The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild


The player controls Link, who wakes up from a 100-year sleep and tries to save the kingdom of Hyrule from Calamity Ganon.

20: Call of Duty: Black Ops 4


It’s the 15th in the Call of Duty series, following up as the sequel to 2015’s Black Ops III. It was Activision’s top digital launch when it came out, grossing more than $500 million worldwide in just the first three days.
